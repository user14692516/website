About Us

Welcome to Syokimau Preparatory School, a place where young minds flourish and discover the joy of learning. Our commitment to excellence in early childhood education is unwavering, and we take pride in providing an environment that nurtures creativity, curiosity, and a love for learning.

Dive into a world of passion and collaboration within our school's diverse array of clubs. From academic pursuits to creative outlets, there's a club for every interest, fostering friendships and fostering talents along the way.They include;

  • Skating
  • Swimming
  • Balet
  • Taekwondo

Our Facilities

At our school, we pride ourselves on providing a warm, welcoming, and enriching environment where children embark on an exciting journey of discovery, creativity, and growth. Nestled in a vibrant community, our facilities are thoughtfully designed to meet the unique needs of young learners, fostering a sense of belonging, curiosity, and joy in every child.

Our Classrooms{A Canva For Imagination}

Step into our classrooms, and you'll find vibrant spaces brimming with colors, textures, and possibilities. Each room is carefully curated to inspire young minds, with ample natural light streaming in to create an inviting atmosphere for learning. Child-sized furniture and age-appropriate materials are strategically arranged to encourage exploration, collaboration, and independent discovery

Outdoor Spaces

Explore our vibrant outdoor spaces designed to ignite young imaginations and foster a love for nature at our kindergarten. From lush gardens and sensory paths to engaging play areas, our outdoor environments provide endless opportunities for exploration, creativity, and active learning. Watch as children flourish in a setting that encourages curiosity, teamwork, and a deep connection with the natural world.

Administration Offices

At our school, the administrative team plays a crucial role in ensuring the smooth and efficient operation of our educational institution. Comprising dedicated professionals with a passion for early childhood education, our administration is committed to providing exemplary leadership, support, and guidance to our staff, students, and families.

Safety Measures

The safety and well-being of every child entrusted to our care are our highest priorities. That's why we've implemented comprehensive safety and security measures throughout our facilities, including secure perimeter fencing, controlled access points, and vigilant supervision by trained staff members. From regular fire drills and emergency preparedness protocols to stringent hygiene practices and sanitation routines, we leave no stone unturned in ensuring a safe, clean, and nurturing environment for all.

Transport

Our transportation services are designed to provide convenient, efficient, and secure transportation options for students, allowing them to arrive at our facility ready to learn, explore, and grow.

Clubs

At our school, we believe that education extends far beyond the confines of the classroom walls. That's why we offer a diverse range of co-curricular activities designed to complement and enhance the academic curriculum, providing students with opportunities to explore their interests, develop new skills, and foster a love for learning that extends beyond traditional subjects.They include Swimming,Skating,Taekwondo and Balet
